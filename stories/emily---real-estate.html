Flint real estate

The effect of crisis on Flint's houses
and homes

By Emily Niedermeyer

Taking into account Flint’s history of redlining, deindustrialization following General Motors’ departure, the late 2000s recession and the water crisis, what is the state of the City’s real estate market? Do poverty levels and population reflect property values? Are developers and investors changing the landscape and are there really streets of abandoned homes?
