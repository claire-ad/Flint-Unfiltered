is a second-year English and journalism major at Northeastern. She was born and raised in Boston, with Moroccan and Iraqi parents. Growing up, Alaa always had a strong passion for writing. Alaa pursued this major in hopes to become a news correspondent to shed light on global and national events that do not get enough coverage. Through her work, she aims to amplify the voices of those who may be often overlooked.
